Pizza della Casa

Fettuccine Alfredo con Pollo

Pizza is one of Italy’s most beloved dishes, made with a simple yeasted dough that bakes into a crisp yet chewy crust, topped with tangy tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and fresh toppings of your choice.

Whether kept classic with just basil and cheese or customized with vegetables, meats, and herbs, pizza is versatile, comforting, and perfect for sharing. Baked until golden and bubbling, it delivers a perfect balance of crispy crust, gooey cheese, and savory flavors in every slice.

Ingredients
  • For the Dough:
    • 500 g (4 cups) all-purpose flour
    • 325 ml (1 ⅓ cups) warm water
    • 2 tsp instant yeast
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • For the Sauce:
    • 1 can (400 g / 14 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • For the Toppings:
    • 250 g (2 ½ cups) shredded mozzarella cheese (or fresh mozzarella slices)
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Olive oil, for drizzling
    • Optional: sliced pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives
Steps
  1. Make the Dough:
    1. In a bowl, mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
    2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
    3. Knead on a floured surface for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    4. Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rise 1–2 hours until doubled in size.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
    2. Add garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
    3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper.
    4. Simmer for 15 minutes, then set aside to cool slightly.
  3. Shape the Dough:
    1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
    2. Punch down risen dough and divide into 2–3 portions (for medium pizzas).
    3. On a floured surface, roll or stretch each portion into a thin round base.
  4. Assemble the Pizza:
    1. Place rolled dough onto a baking sheet or pizza peel.
    2. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the base.
    3. Sprinkle evenly with mozzarella cheese.
    4. Add fresh basil and any desired toppings.
    5. Drizzle lightly with olive oil.
  5. Bake:
    1. Transfer pizza to oven (or onto preheated pizza stone).
    2. Bake for 10–12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  6. Serve:
    1. Remove from oven and let cool slightly.
    2. Slice and serve warm.
